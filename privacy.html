Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Discord bot ("the Bot"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Important: The Bot only processes Discord identifiers (IDs) necessary for functionality. We do not collect, store, or process personal information such as email addresses, IP addresses, or message content.

1. Data Collected

The Bot processes the following types of Discord identifiers:

  • User IDs: Discord user identifiers used for role assignment and team management
  • Server IDs: Discord server identifiers used for server-specific configuration
  • Role IDs: Discord role identifiers used for role management and permissions
  • Channel IDs: Discord channel identifiers used for channel creation and management

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Message content or conversation data
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Payment information
  • Any other sensitive personal data

2. Data Usage

The collected Discord IDs are used exclusively for:

  • Providing team management functionality
  • Assigning and managing roles within Discord servers
  • Creating and configuring channels
  • Providing server utility functions
  • Maintaining server-specific configurations

We do not use this data for advertising, marketing, or any purposes other than providing the Bot's core functionality.

3. Data Storage

Discord IDs are stored locally in configuration files on the server hosting the Bot. This data is:

  • Stored securely and accessed only for Bot functionality
  • Not shared with third parties
  • Not used for any purpose other than Bot operations
  • Subject to automatic deletion when the Bot leaves a server (see Data Removal section)

We do not maintain external databases or cloud storage systems for this data.

4. Data Removal

You have the right to request the removal of your data at any time. Data removal occurs automatically in the following scenarios:

  • Bot Removal: When the Bot is removed from a Discord server, all server-specific data is automatically deleted
  • User Request: Users can request data removal by contacting the Bot's support channels
  • Account Deletion: If a Discord account is deleted, associated IDs become invalid and are no longer processed

To request manual data removal, please contact us through the Bot's support server or official channels.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the data we process:

  • Configuration files are stored securely on the server
  • Access to data is restricted to Bot operations only
  • Regular security reviews and updates are performed

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

The Bot operates within Discord's platform and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We do not share data with third-party services beyond what is necessary for Discord integration.

7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through appropriate channels, such as the Bot's support server or official documentation.

Your continued use of the Bot after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights regarding your data, please contact us through:

  • The Bot's support server
  • Official documentation channels
